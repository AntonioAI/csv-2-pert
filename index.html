PERT Chart Generator

Transform your project tasks from a CSV file into a professional PERT diagram for draw.io.

How it Works:

  1. Prepare Your CSV: Ensure your CSV has columns: task_id, description, optimistic_time, most_likely_time, pessimistic_time, and dependencies. (Show Details)
  2. Upload: Click "Choose CSV File" and select your prepared file.
  3. Generate: Hit the "Generate PERT XML" button.
  4. Import: Download the XML and import it into draw.io to visualize your PERT chart!

Tip for 'dependencies' column: If a task has multiple dependencies (e.g., TaskA, TaskB), enclose them in quotes in your CSV: "TaskA,TaskB". Single dependencies don't strictly need quotes.
